Standard Terms and Cost

Interest Rates

  • Our Business Finance Annual Interest Rates range from 8.95% to 18.95% per annum.
  • The interest rate that is applicable to the particular client will be based on their risk profile such as credit history, industry, business trading records, financial obligations, asset backing, security value offered and loan amount.

Default Interest

  • If the Borrower fails to make a payment on the due date or any outstanding moneys payable by the Borrower, we may charge the Borrower Default Interest.
  • The Annual Default Interest Rate is the Annual Interest Rate plus 10% per annum.
  • Default Interest is charged on the amount of any missed payment from the time Borrower fails to make the due payment until time of actual payment made.
